About the B-Tech CS Program

The Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science (B-Tech CS) is a four-year undergraduate program that provides in-depth knowledge and skills in computer science and engineering. It is designed for students aiming to build a strong foundation in various areas of computer science, from software development to data science and artificial intelligence.
